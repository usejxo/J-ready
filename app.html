Before You Begin

Please make sure you are logged into I‑Ready.

Injection Instructions

1. Open the I‑Ready page: I‑Ready Login.
2. Once the page loads, right-click and select “Inspect” (or use a two‐finger tap on your touchpad) and switch to the Console tab.
3. Click inside the code box below, press Ctrl+A to select all, then Ctrl+C to copy the script.
4. Paste it into the Console (using Ctrl+V) and press Enter.
5. A GUI will appear on the I‑Ready page displaying the status of both injected scripts. (If you don’t see it, press Ctrl+Shift+J to open Developer Tools.)
